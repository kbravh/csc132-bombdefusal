Keep Talking and Nobody Explodes

On the Subject of Cutting the Wires

One wrong snip leads you one step closer to explosion!

  • You are given the choice to cut as many of the 3 wires needed
  • The wires needed to be cut are based upon the serial number either:
    • Ending with an odd digit
    • Ending with an even digit

    • If the serial number ends with an odd digit:

    • You must cut both wires 1 and 3.

    If the serial number ends with an even digit:

    • You must cut both wires 1 and 2.

Keep Talking and Nobody Explodes The Big Button

On the Subject of the Big Button

Such a large and bright button! So tempting to press over and over...

Follow these given instructions in order to avoid certain detonation.

The "big button" has a light which shows colors such as:

  • RED
  • BLUE
  • GREEN

If the color shown is red:

  • You must press the button when the timer reaches an EVEN number.

If the color shown is blue:

  • You must press the button when the timer reaches an ODD number.

If the color shown is green:

  • You must press the button when the timer reaches a multiple of THREE.
